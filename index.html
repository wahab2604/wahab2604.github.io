About Me

Passionate about materials science and programming

I am a cement researcher with extensive experience in high temperature chemistry and thermodynamics. My work focuses on understanding the fundamental processes that govern cement production and performance.

Beyond my research in materials science, I am enthusiastic about programming, particularly in Python. I leverage computational tools to analyze data, model chemical processes, and develop solutions that bridge the gap between traditional materials science and modern data-driven approaches.

This website serves as a repository of my work and interesting discoveries in both materials science and programming. It's a continuous work in progress as I expand my skills and knowledge.
